Top Brands

1. SUSHI (Japanese)

Made with vinegared rice and usually topped or filled with fresh seafood like tuna, salmon, or shrimp. Sushi is known for its precise preparation and presentation, emphasizing the natural flavors of the ingredients.

2. HAMBURGER (American)

A grilled or fried ground beef patty served in a bun, typically topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments like ketchup or mustard. Hamburgers are a symbol of American fast food culture, known for their juicy meat and customizable toppings.

3.VINDALOO(Indian)

Originating from Goa, Vindaloo is a spicy curry dish made with marinated meat (usually pork or lamb) cooked with vinegar, red chilies, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and mustard seeds. It's known for its fiery heat and tangy flavor.

4. LAAL MAAS

A traditional Rajasthani dish featuring tender mutton cooked in a spicy red chili paste along with garlic, yogurt, and aromatic spices. It's known for its intense spiciness balanced with rich flavors.

5. PRAWN BALCHAO

Another Goan specialty, Prawn Balchao is a spicy and tangy pickle-like dish made with prawns cooked in a fiery red chili paste, vinegar, and spices. It's often served as a side dish or with rice.
